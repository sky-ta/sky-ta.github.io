LivingRoom

a personal online archive of encouraging voice messages

About

LivingRoom is a personal online archive of encouraging voice messages in the form of a mobile website. Now more than ever, connecting with your loved one through distance/distancing and across time zones has become a relevant part of many people's lives. With LivingRoom, you can start a personal collection of positive affirmations from your loved ones.

This website is NOT an emergency intervention solution. If you or a loved one is in danger, refer to this hotlines resource or your local emergency hotlines.
